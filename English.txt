===== Main Grammar =====

* Present Perfect
	* keywords:
		* just
		* already
		* for/since
		* ever
		* never
		* yet
		* how
			* much
			* many
			* often
		* up till now
	* use
		* happened in recent past, effect on now
		* unfinished time
		* for/since
		* no V-ing form 
	* e.g.
		* I've known my best friend since school.
* Present Perfect Continous
	* keywords:
		* for/since
		* how long... (up till now)
	* use
		* started in the past and it's not over
		* used with
			* 'how long...?'
			* 'for/since'
	* e.g.
		* I've been libing in Sistrans since 2014
