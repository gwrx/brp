The situation:
You are a consultant from "Go Green", an agency that helps businesses becomre more environmentally friendly. You have been aske to give a presentation to the managers of McDonalds in Tirol (=examiners) on how they can make their restaurants more environmentally friendly.

Part 1:
* Give a presentation on tips for how McDonalds restaurants can be more environmentally friendly
	* cook to order
	* reduce usage of fat
	* eatable packaging (i.e. for ice cream)
* Use your own ideas as well as ideas from the attached text "The body shop"


* Introduction
	* You are not green enough!
	* Who I am
	* What we do
	* What I am talking about
		* 3 Ideas to get greener
* Body
	* Idea 1: cook to order → no more wasting good food
		* reduce waste by 75% instantly
		* customers will wait for good quality & fresh food
	* Idea 2: reducing fat → use hot air to cook
		* less fat → more healthy → more customers
		* more sells: feeling of beeing full comes later
	* Idea 3: eatable packaging → rice paper; ice cream waffels
		* love the waffels
			* tastey, crunchy
		* get them back! pretty please!
* Conclusion
	* Thank you
	* questions?

===== The Exam =====
Part A: Q & A (1-2 min) (work & study, past, present, future)

Part 1: Monologe (3-4 min) (roleplay)
Part 2: Dialogue (3-4 min) (roleplay)
Part D: Q & A (all examiners)

=== Example Practise Matura ===

"The working world" - Telecommuting (Working from Home)

The situation:
Your CEO from Neww Zealand (=examiner) wants to reduce the number of workers in the main office, and offer employees the option of working from home. She has called a meeting with you, as the COO of the company, to hear your opinion on the proposal.

Part 1: Monologue (3-min talk)
Your Task: Firstly, outline:
* the problems your employees could face when they work from home
* solutions to these problems
(to cover thes points use information from the attached text "Staying Sane While Working from Home", as well as your own ideas.)

Part 2: Dialogue
You then have a discussion with your CEO about the adantages and disadvantages of offering staff the option of telecommuting.
Your Task: Be prepared to consider:
* which staff members might be interested in working from home and why
* the positives having some staff working from home would have for the company
* the negatives the change could have for the company
* possible solutions to these problems

Part 3: Follow-up Discussion
Be prepared to answer some questions about the working world from the examiners.

Please note: It is advisable to make notes during the preparationn time, but do not write full sentences. You can look at your notes when giving your talk an participating in the dialogue.

=== Example ===

* Problems they face
	* no boundaries in their home
	* no fixed times, no rythm
	* homeworkers are hard to communicate with
	* no real check on efficiency of their work
	* hard to get a work-community running
	* home-workers are not suitable for dealing directly with customers

* Solutions to the problems:
	* weekly meetings with all
	* daily tele-meetings with their supervisor
	* giving them advise how to set boundaries
	* only suitable for 50-75% worktime employees
	* don't let them overwork - educate the supervisors
	* it has to be their free decision
